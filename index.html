About

Andrew Koval is a Kyiv, Ukraine-born traveller, photographer and programmer basen in Merseburg, Germany. After 2 years in Kyiv Polytechnical University he took part in Double-Diploma Programme with Hochschule Merseburg, University of Applied Scinces and moved to Germany. In 2017 he got a degree of Bachelor of Sciences in Computer Science. Now he is studying Applied Informatics in Germany.

His passion is Mountains and Photography.
